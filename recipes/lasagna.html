Lasagna

This is lasagna recipe with a meaty, made from scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground pork, ½ pound lean ground beef.
  • A diced onion is cooked until translucent with the ground meat.
  • A can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes.
  • two tablespoons of parsley and crush one clove of garlic.
  • A dash of sugar balances.
  • Dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  • The lasagna noodles.
  • The cheese layer is made up of cottage cheese and Parmesan.
  • Eggs make the cheese layer extra creamy.

Steps

  1. Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly.
  2. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent.
  3. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  4. Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  5. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  6. Layer the ingredients (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  7. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour.
  8. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
  9. Should be perfectly baked in about 50 minutes (30-40 minutes covered, 5-10 minutes uncovered).
